About me

I have completed my postgraduation (Int. M.Sc. in Physics) from the Centre for Excellence in Basic Sciences, Mumbai. I was awarded Chanakya-PG Fellow at University of Hyderabad to carryout my research endeavours after graduation. I am deepely fascinated by the light and its behaviour. I am interested in taming light properties and exploring its possible applications. I am an experimetalist by nature and can also take on computational tasks. My preferred specialisations include Experimental Quantum optics, Light-matter interaction and Experiments related to Quantum technology. I also have keen interest in floquent systems that can be modulated by light. Time crystals and Ultra Cold atoms physics are other topics which interests me. I have also developed interest in Many-body Physics and Atom-interferometry. Looking forward to working and exploring these beautiful topics.
